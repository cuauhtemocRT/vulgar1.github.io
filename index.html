THE BAND


Vulgar Addiction is an independent metal band whose trajectory is characterized by constant evolution within genres such as Death, Black, and Thrash Metal. Since their inception, they have managed to position themselves in the Mexican scene through self-managed releases and participation in highly relevant festivals.

Below are the key aspects of their history and career:

Milestones and Recognition The band has garnered various achievements over the years, highlighting their technical capacity and stage presence:

• Beginnings:
Won second place in the "War of Bands" contest at Circo Volador, which allowed them to record their first EP, Reflejo de Odio.
• Competitions:
Were semifinalists in the "WOA Mexico Battle 2013".
• Kalani Awards:
Have been recognized on multiple occasions, winning "The Best Thrash Metal Band" award in 2019 and receiving a nomination for "Death Metal Band of the Year" in 2015.
• Indie-O Music Awards:
In 2015, their album Pulso Neuroviral was nominated for "Best Metal Album of the Year".

Official Discography The band has maintained a constant production of long-play (LP) and extended-play (EP) material independently: Year Title Format 2007 Reflejo de Odio EP 2014 Pulso Neuroviral LP 2017 D-Hominizacion LP 2024 Figures in Black LP Live Presence and Videography Vulgar Addiction has extensive experience on national stages, sharing the bill with international bands like Destruction and participating in the Hell & Heaven festival in its 10th anniversary edition. Additionally, they have produced various official videos to promote their music, including tracks like "666", "Metralla", "Mortui Docent", and their most recent material from 2024, "Homo Umbra".
